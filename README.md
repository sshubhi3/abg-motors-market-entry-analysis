# abg-motors-market-entry-analysis
Market entry analysis for ABG Motors (India vs Japan) using data analytics

📌 ABG Motors – India Market Entry Analysis
🧩 Problem Statement

ABG Motors, a Japanese automobile manufacturer, is evaluating entry into the Indian market.
The objective is to determine whether the company can realistically achieve 10,000 annual vehicle sales and identify the optimal entry strategy.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze automobile demand trends in India vs Japan
Identify high-growth vehicle segments
Evaluate competitive landscape
Estimate feasibility of achieving target sales
Provide data-driven market entry recommendation
🗂️ Data Sources
India automobile sales data (2020–2024)
Japan automobile sales data (2020–2024)
Macro indicators (GDP, population, car ownership)
Competitor market share data
🛠️ Tools Used
Power BI – Dashboard & visualization
PostgreSQL – Data querying
Excel – Data preparation
📊 Key Insights
India shows strong growth, while Japan is a saturated market
SUV segment dominates (~45%), making it the ideal entry point
Car ownership gap is massive (India ~30 vs Japan ~600 per 1000 people)
Competition is concentrated but fragmented, allowing new entrants
Only ~0.2–0.3% market share needed to achieve 10,000 units/year
📈 Dashboard

🧠 Final Recommendation

ABG Motors should enter the Indian market with a focus on the Compact SUV segment.
The required market share to achieve 10,000 annual sales is minimal, making entry feasible and strategically attractive.

🚀 Business Impact
Identifies high-growth segment (SUVs)
Quantifies achievable sales target
Supports data-driven market entry decision
Reduces strategic risk
